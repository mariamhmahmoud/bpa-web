NATIONAL PARKS

Photo Credit: "Barthodi Park and Fountain." Architect of the Capitol, www.aoc.gov/capitol-buildings/bartholdi-park-and-fountain.

Georgetown Waterfront Park

If you're looking for a day full of fun and sun, come visit Georgetown Waterfront Park, where you can spend time splashing in the fountain, or enjoy the riverfront's beautiful landscape. Filled with open green space, and serene walkways, this is one place you don't want to miss.

Cost: FREE

Hours: 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. Monday-Saturday, Closed Sunday

Address: 3303 Water St NW, Washington, DC 20007

More Information Here

Rock Creek Park

Do you love the outdoors? Get away from the hustle and bustle of D.C. and come explore Rock Creek Park, filled with hiking trails, interesting history, and more! Stop by and visit this beautiful slice of peace.

Cost: FREE

Hours: Open during daylight hours and closed during night hours

Address: Washington, DC 20008

More Information Here

Theodore Roosevelt Island

Love to hike? Consider exploring the Theodore Roosevelt Island, filled with walking trails and beautiful woodlands, where one can not only enjoy nature's magnificence, but also see memorials honoring our nation's 26th president.

Trails:

  • Swamp trail: 1.5 miles
  • Woods Trail: .33 miles
  • Upland Trail: .75 miles

Cost: FREE

Hours: 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. daily

Address: None - can only be reached through George Washington Memorial Parkway

More Information Here

U.S. Botanic Garden

Enjoy the U.S. Botanic Garden, filled with breathtaking flora and all sorts of living artworks from across the world. Admire the ever-growing exhibits and the beautiful floral artifacts in the museum.

Cost: FREE

Hours:

  • Conservatory: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. daily
  • National Garden: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. daily
  • Bartholdi Park: dawn to dusk daily

Address: 100 Maryland Ave SW, Washington, DC 20001

More Information Here

U.S. National Arboretum

Visit the U.S National Arboretum to view the breathtaking simplicity of nature. This place is a great place for die-hard plant lovers to people who just simply want to enjoy the majestic view.

Cost: FREE

Hours: 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. daily

Address: 3501 New York Ave NE, Washington, DC 20002
