TRIP PLANNER

To make planning your trip a little bit easier, we provided a trip planner below. We also included suggestions of what to put in your trip planner, so copy and paste them into the planner text box if you like them and click "Add" (or you can type whatever you wish to do into the box)! Also, if you know you will not be using certain suggestions and want to shorten the list of suggestions, double click the suggestion and it will go away. Clicking out of this page will delete your planner, so make sure you open any new pages in a new window.
